Describe, in words, what the equation $\Delta G = \Delta H - T \Delta S$ means

Summary

  • $\Delta G$ measures how likely one state is relative to others (how often the robot/peptide is in that state).
  • $\Delta H$ describes the favorability of interactions within that state (the depth of the hole)
  • $\Delta S$ describes the difference in the number of conformations between two states (the relative areas of each state)

Biochemical reactions are always tradeoffs between enthalpy and entropy
